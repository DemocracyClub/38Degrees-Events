Welcome to the 38 Degrees election hub

From Saving our NHS, to tackling tax dodgers and stopping TTIP, 38 Degrees members across the country are working together to make sure that this election is fought on the issues we all care about.

Fill in your postcode to get started. You'll then need to add your details and create a password. This is so you can log in and see events near you and chat with other local 38 Degrees members. (you’ll be asked for the password every time you visit the website). If you've already registered, please click 'Sign in' at the top of this page.

Already, thousands of us have signed up to meet other 38 Degrees members and hold 'Save our NHS' events on high streets across England. But if we’re really going to make a difference, more of us need to volunteer. Fill in your postcode below to get involved and chat with other members near you.
